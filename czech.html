Czech Republic

Prague; the city so beautiful that is was spared by Hitler...or so the story goes. Prague is a living and breathing enigma; old and dirty but somehow unimaginably graceful.

World of Wanderlust

Prague

At this point in our lives, Prague had been the farthest we’d been from home. We didn’t know what to expect out of Eastern Europe. We were told Prague would be beautiful, but until you visit for yourself, you really have no idea. The detail in the buildings is inspiring, a city that was spared during WWII, Prague has looked this beautiful for many years. Getting up close to Prague castle is worth the trip alone.

I found the food to be amazing in Prague, Marissa on the other hand had difficulties due to her vegetarian ways. Massive portions of pork, succulent roast duck, traditional Czech dumplings and goulash just to name a few were spectacular. Prague is also know for offering some of the best beer in the world, Pilsner being invented in the town of Pilsen, Czech Republic. Based on significant personal experience, we can speak confidently of it’s greatness and affordability.
